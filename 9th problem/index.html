Frequently Asked Questions

  • What services does Vaddi Bank offer?
    Vaddi Bank provides secure money transactions, savings accounts, loans, and investment options tailored to your financial needs.
  • How can I open an account?
    You can open an account online through our website or visit any of our branches with valid ID and proof of address.
  • Is my money safe with Vaddi Bank?
    Absolutely! We use state-of-the-art encryption and security protocols to ensure your funds and data are protected at all times.
  • What are the fees for money transfers?
    Our fees depend on the transfer amount and destination. Please refer to our fees schedule on the website or contact customer support for detailed info.
  • How do I reset my online banking password?
    You can reset your password by clicking the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and following the instructions sent to your registered email.
