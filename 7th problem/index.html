Vaddi Bank

Our History

Vaddi Bank was founded in 2015 by visionary entrepreneur Sunny Vaddi. Starting as a small financial startup, Vaddi Bank has rapidly become one of India’s trusted names in money transactions and financial services.

Our commitment to innovative digital solutions has enabled millions of customers to send and receive money securely and efficiently across the country.

Our Mission

At Vaddi Bank, our mission is to revolutionize the way money transactions are done — making them faster, safer, and accessible to everyone.

We aim to bridge the financial divide through cutting-edge technology, customer-focused service, and unwavering integrity.

Our Team

Led by founder Sunny Vaddi and co-founder Navyaa, our talented team comprises financial experts, software engineers, and customer care professionals dedicated to delivering exceptional service.

We cultivate a culture of innovation, trust, and inclusiveness — driving Vaddi Bank forward to meet the evolving needs of our customers.
